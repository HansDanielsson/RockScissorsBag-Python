# RockScissorsBag-Python

The game of simple Rock-Scissors-Bag

The computer randomly chooses which of rock, scissors, or bag to choose.

路 The player also chooses rock, scissors, or bag.

路 The computer and the player then show their choices simultaneously.

路 The rules are as follows: rock wins over scissors, scissors wins over bag, and bag wins over rock. If both choose the same option, it is a draw.

路 The player plays until he wins or loses against the computer.
